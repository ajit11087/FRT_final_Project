About Us

Help us to help You, Be the one to make a change!

Is it fair that millions of people in a handful of high-income countries have received the vast majority of vaccines before others? In an equal world, during a global health emergency, protecting the vulnerable and saving lives should happen in unity. You can be part of ending the pandemic for us all. As the world confronts its biggest challenge in living memory, there has never been a greater need for accurate, verified information. Like the virus itself, misinformation spreads from person-to-person, heightening the risk to health and spreading fear and division. The world cannot contain the disease and its impacts without access to trusted, accurate information that promotes science and real solutions – and builds solidarity within and between nations.
